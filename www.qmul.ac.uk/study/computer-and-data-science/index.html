Computer and Data Science

The diversity and quality of our research will provide you with a rich understanding of the exciting world of computer science
Apply now - undergraduate courses Apply now - postgraduate courses

We are proud of our 129-year history. Our enthusiasm for discovery defines our courses, and commercial industry partnerships are well established. We offer courses that are a well-considered mix of core modules and exciting electives.
