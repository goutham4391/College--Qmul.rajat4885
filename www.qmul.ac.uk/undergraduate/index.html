Access and Participation Plan

Our Access and Participation Plan sets out our commitment to widening participation and includes the measures we have put in place around outreach work and support for students from widening participation backgrounds. Access and participation plans are approved by the Director of Fair Access and Participation at the Office for Students. You can view a copy of our latest plan on our Queen Mary Access and Particpation Plan page or the Office for Students webpage.
