A smart toothpaste that “knows” what your teeth need

Researchers from the Institute of Dentistry and the School of Engineering and Materials Science have collaborated to develop an award-winning ‘smart’ toothpaste. BioMinF uses patented bioactive glass technology to protect teeth from decay and reduce sensitivity and pain. The toothpastes, launched in 2016, are now on sale in over 20 countries globally, with up to 100,000 tubes being produced every month.
